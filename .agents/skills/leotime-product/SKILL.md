# leotime Product

Keep the product small, personal, and understandable.

## Product Principles

- Fast daily time capture.
- Calendar and timesheet views matter.
- Reporting and invoicing matter from the start.
- Offline work matters, but full local-first sync is backlog.
- Docker-first VPS deployment is a product requirement.
- Documentation is part of the product.

## Decision Rules

- Prefer boring architecture when it improves maintainability.
- Do not add SaaS/team complexity until the single-owner workflow is strong.
- Document tradeoffs in `docs/adr` when architecture changes.
- Keep Spanish and English in mind for UI-facing text.
